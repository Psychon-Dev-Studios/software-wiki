About

CamX: Rebirth is a FNaF fan game which places you in the role of a night guard. Surrounded by animatronics out for your blood, and with limited power, your only defenses are two weak lights, two doors, and a camera system. You have to survive until 6AM to escape, and you must do this for an entire week.


Game Mechanics

Office

The office is pretty simple. It contains the doors, four buttons (two to toggle the doors and two to toggle the lights), and your camera interface.


Office Doors

The office doors are your last line of defense. If there is something at your door, click the top (red) button on the appropriate side to close the door and lock out the danger. Of course, the door will use valuable power...


Door Lights

Of course, you can't just see out into the halls, they're dark! The bottom button on the door control panels is your door lights. These allow you to see any dangers at your door, at the cost of using valuable power...


Cameras

Your first line of defense is the pizzeria's security cameras. Using your security access panel, you can view every camera in the building, and see where the animatronics are. Of course, they can still move, even with you watching them... And using the cameras will use power...


Power

Power is your most valuable resource. Everything you do will use power... And it's gotta last until 6; you can't get it back! If you run out of power, the entire building will shut down... lights will shut off, cameras will turn off, and your doors will open...
